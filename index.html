About

Basketball is a one of the most popular sport played. It is a two team sport that was invented in 1891 by Canadian Amercian gym teacher James Naismith in Springfield, Massachusetts, United States. Each team will usually have 5 players, sometimes teams might have 4 if there aren't enough players Ever since its inception, it has been a stable and steady but massive improvement with the rise of amazing stars such as Micheal Jordan, Kobe Bryant, and Kareem Al-Jabbar.
